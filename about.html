About me

Viktor Annerstål is an award winning cinematographer based in Stockholm, Sweden. Raised with a DV-camera in his right hand and a skateboard in his left, he early on developed a street-influenced style in cinematography. Right after finishing his studies at KTH royal institute of technology as a Computer hardware engineer he decieded to alter his path and study cinematography and has been behind the lens ever since.
